About KNOWIT

A practical knowledge initiative for digital services

What KNOWIT is

A York-inspired initiative focused on clearer service knowledge, better support pathways, and stronger student technology outcomes.

What it is not

It is not a replacement for service desks, security teams, or academic tools. It complements them with usable guidance.

Guiding principles

  • Clarity before complexity
  • Actionable language over jargon
  • Consistent pathways across channels
  • Continuous improvement from feedback

Intended audiences

Students, staff, instructors, and service support teams who need confident, timely technology guidance.

Contact and feedback

Share suggestions and service pain points with the KNOWIT coordination team at knowit@yorku.ca.
